      Hazara, founded in 1947 by Hukamchand Singh Julka is a hidden gem in Mumbai serving delectable Indian food. Starting from his humble kitchen in Sion-Koliwada, he was instrumental in introducing Mumbai to the famous Prawns and Fish Koliwada - a delightful favorite amongst many. These dishes were first served to guests in 1955 and ever since then have been served to many all over the world. Little did he know then that his humble delicacy would be famous not just in the city but would also make it to the menus of famed restaurants in London and New York. These deep-fried, crunchy dishes are enjoyed till today alongside a glass of chilled beer.
